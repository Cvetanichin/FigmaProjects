Operational Risk Management Methodology

The Purpose of the Matrix

A risk matrix is an assessment tool that allows organizations to define the probability and severity parameters of key hazards. By visualizing threats, leaders can allocate contingency reserves and deploy proactive mitigations.

Interactive Framework Variables

Likelihood (1-3): Represents Probability. High likelihood (3) implies a routine exposure, Medium (2) is occasional, and Low (1) is highly rare.
Impact (1-3): Represents Severity. High severity (3) might collapse workspace functions, Medium (2) causes operational delays, and Low (1) causes minor friction.
