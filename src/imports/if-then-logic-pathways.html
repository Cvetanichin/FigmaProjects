🎯 If-Then Logic Pathways

Theory of Change: From Activities to Impact

🎯 ULTIMATE IMPACT

To protect the well-being, human rights and fundamental freedoms, with focus on girls, women and vulnerable groups in North Macedonia

📰 WP1: Transformative Role of Media
🔹 IF
Journalists receive mentorship on gender stereotypes, produce investigative stories on gender movements, and are trained in ethical gender-sensitive reporting
⬇
🔸 THEN
Media outlets will report more accurately on gender issues, expose disinformation campaigns, and counter harmful stereotypes in their coverage
⬇
🔷 THEREFORE
Public narrative shifts away from harmful gender stereotypes, citizens gain access to fact-based information, and disinformation loses its influence
⬇
🎯 IMPACT CONTRIBUTION
Women, girls, and vulnerable groups are protected from media-driven discrimination and manipulation, strengthening their fundamental freedoms and well-being
⚖️ WP2: Countering Legislative Setback
🔹 IF
Constitutional Court judges participate in international study visits, attend workshops on international human rights legislation, and receive educational materials on gender-based violations
⬇
🔸 THEN
The Constitutional Court will have enhanced capacity to recognize and prosecute gender-based violations, apply international human rights standards, and resist legislative rollbacks on women's and LGBTI rights
⬇
🔷 THEREFORE
Legal protections for vulnerable groups are strengthened and enforced, creating accountability for violence and discrimination through the justice system
⬇
🎯 IMPACT CONTRIBUTION
Constitutional rights of women, girls, and vulnerable groups are safeguarded against backlash, ensuring their fundamental freedoms are legally protected and upheld
🤝 WP3: Access to Services
🔹 IF
Healthcare and social service providers receive training on non-discriminatory approaches, CSOs are trained in psychosocial/medical/legal assistance, and local services receive sub-grants in three municipalities
⬇
🔸 THEN
Community-based services that are non-discriminatory, affordable, and responsive to specific needs of LGBTI, women, and children become available in local municipalities
⬇
🔷 THEREFORE
Vulnerable and marginalized groups can access essential healthcare, social support, legal aid, and psychosocial services without facing discrimination or barriers
⬇
🎯 IMPACT CONTRIBUTION
Inequalities are reduced as women, girls, LGBTI persons, and vulnerable groups gain practical access to services that protect their well-being and fundamental rights
📢 WP4: Communication & Awareness
🔹 IF
Journalistic articles are published, social media campaigns expose fake narratives, and conferences/local advocacy meetings engage rights-holders and duty-bearers in three municipalities
⬇
🔸 THEN
The general public, rights-holders, and duty-bearers gain accurate knowledge about gender-based violence, gender diversity, sexual education, and how to recognize and respond to threats
⬇
🔷 THEREFORE
Misconceptions about gender and gender identity are dismantled, creating a more informed public that actively supports rather than threatens the rights of vulnerable groups
⬇
🎯 IMPACT CONTRIBUTION
Social attitudes shift to protect rather than harm vulnerable groups, creating an enabling environment for the fundamental freedoms and well-being of women, girls, and LGBTI persons
